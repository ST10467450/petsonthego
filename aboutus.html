About Us

Founded in 2023, PetsOnTheGo is a fast-growing pet care company focused on keeping pets healthy, happy, and well cared for while giving owners peace of mind. We provide reliable services such as grooming, training, walking, and tailored care for both common and exotic pets. At PetsOnTheGo, we treat every pet like family and are committed to offering compassionate, trustworthy care. With convenience at the heart of what we do, we continue to be the go-to choice for pet owners who want only the best for their companions.

Our mission: To deliver accessible, reliable, and top-quality pet care services to households everywhere and provide valuable training to pet owners.

Our vision: To establish PetsOnTheGo as the leading nationwide hub for pet care solutions and information.
